Empowering Innovation and Technology in Africa: The Akure Tech Hub Story

Akure Tech Hub is a community-driven workspace located in the heart of Akure, the capital city of Ondo State in Nigeria. The hub was established in response to the growing need for a supportive environment for technology and social innovations in under-served African communities.

One of the primary objectives of Akure Tech Hub is to support small businesses and upcoming entrepreneurs. The hub has designed a package where entrepreneurs can have a workspace in their facility for as low as 1050 naira per day, which gives them access to unlimited Wi-Fi, open events, and a whole lot more. The workspace is fully fitted with furniture, which gives entrepreneurs and business owners the opportunity to own the space on a monthly plan at affordable rates.

Akure Tech Hub has a diverse community of Biochemists, electrical engineers, sociologists, lawyers, software developers, business developers, aeronautical and space engineers, chefs, accountants, graphic artists, web designers, and more. This diverse community pool has been able to harness their individual strengths stemming from their years of experience, professional background, and passion to build an impact-oriented society.

In addition to fostering collaboration and innovation within its community, Akure Tech Hub collaborates with relevant stakeholders and community members to pioneer innovative approaches to solving problems through the power of technology. The hub has partnered with over 20 organizations this year alone to develop innovative solutions to societal problems.

During the design phase of the workspace, the team at Akure Tech Hub had one goal in mind - to design a workspace that was beyond the face value of aesthetics, so that as people interacted with the space, they could and would feel inspired. In a time where everything is becoming virtual, the hub believes it is even more important for people to continue connecting physically in meaningful settings. The Akure Tech Hub is designed to provide an inspirational place to work and is a focal point for change within the local community.

In conclusion, the Akure Tech Hub is a community-driven workspace that provides a platform for entrepreneurs, innovators, and creative individuals to collaborate and build impactful solutions to societal problems through the power of technology. The hub's diverse community pool and collaborative partnerships make it a prime location for fostering innovation and creating positive change within the community.
