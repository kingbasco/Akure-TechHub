Get to know us

Akure Tech Hub

Akure tech hub was established as a collaborative synergy between Emerging Communities Africa, The Ondo State Government (ODSG) and The Federal University of Technology, Akure (FUTA) to champion the advancement of social innovation and grassroots development, thereby addressing the pressing challenges encountered by underserved communities.

Our Communities

Creatives Community

Join our community of graphic designers, brand identity specialists, content writers, content creators, video editors, technical writers, storytellers, and more.

Techies Community

Community for tech leaders and newcomers. All welcome - developers, engineers, and tech enthusiasts.

Business Community

Our community is for dedicated individuals driving business success and impact. Join us today if you're an entrepreneur, professional or interested in the field, and find a supportive network.

Agric Community

Our community is for those dedicated to making a difference in agriculture - farmers, researchers, and sustainability enthusiasts. Join us for a supportive network in Agribusiness.

Mechatrics Community

We are a group focused on mechatronics, bringing together experts from different backgrounds to collaborate and advance the field. Join us if you're passionate and curious about mechanical and electrical engineering, and computer science.

Blockchain/Crypto Community

Join our community of like-minded individuals who share your passion for decentralized technology and digital currencies, or if you're looking to enter the Web3 space.

Data Analytics Community

Our community is for individuals dedicated to unlocking the potential of data and making data-driven decisions.

Hernovate Community

The hernovate Community is a community for women across different skillset and vocations.
